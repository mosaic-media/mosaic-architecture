# Glossary

---

# Alert

An operator-facing notification that human attention may be required.

---

# Diagnostic

A safe operational view of current Platform structure or state.

---

# Health

A statement about whether a component can currently fulfil its architectural responsibility.

---

# Operational Readiness

The condition in which a component can be observed, diagnosed and supported without implementation knowledge.

---

# Safe Debugging

Debugging that explains behaviour without changing production behaviour or bypassing authority.
